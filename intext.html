About Us

We are an organization that connects (cancer patients, addicts, etc.) with survivors and other (patients) to provide a network of support, motivation, and hope of life after recovery. The stories, struggles, and triumphs from survivors, as well as inspiring proverbs are relayed to active members in daily text messages to regularly remind members that there is hope for a better life ahead of them. Sign up with just a phone number to receive messages and start today on your path to a better future.
